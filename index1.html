JÖNKÖPING
Fun facts
  • Jönköping is the 10th biggest city in Sweden
  • Jönköping has 144,000 inhabitants
  • Jönköping is situated on the southern shore of Sweden's second largest lake, Vättern.
  • Jönköping is an old trading centre situated at a natural crossroads for routes following the rivers Nissan and Lagan.
What to do

Tändstickssområdet

Tändstickssområdet in Jönköping is a beautiful and historic place to experience all year round. In this area there are shops and restaurants of really high quality. Here you will find celebrated restaurants such as the fish restaurant Pescadores, Restaurant Tegel and the beer bar Idlewild. Stroll through the Ateljen store, a place for events, photo shoots, conferences. In the heart of the Tändsticks area is the Tändsticksmuseet, one of three museums in the whole world that tells the story of the famous match.

Kayaking

Kayaking A refreshing way to experience Jönköping is to see the city from the water, and why not from a kayak? Paddle out on either Lake Vättern or Munksjön for a full or half day. Perfect activity to do while on vacation with someone you care about. You can easily rent a kayak in central Jönköping at Outdoor days.

Apple valley

Stroll among lengths of apple orchards, have coffee in a beautiful setting or buy locally produced ingredients with you. Äppledalen is a fantastic place to take a day trip to in any season. Here you will find, among other things, Rudenstams Gårdsbutik & Cafe, which offers local spirits, cider, vegetables and other goodies.

The city park

On the west side of Jönköping you will find the beautiful Stadparken, which offers a unique view of the city. In addition to a beautiful view, you will find here a café, a large playground, dance floor, historical monuments and many animals that you can visit.

Sports

If you are intrested in sport, depending on which season it is when you are visiting, you can go watch HV71 play icehochey in the highest legue, or Jsödra playing soccer.

Spira

Kulturhuset Spira is a cultural center mainly for the performing arts in Jönköping, inaugurated on 11 November 2011. In the building, Småland's Music and Theater puts on stage performances in several genres with around 800 concerts and other performances per year.
